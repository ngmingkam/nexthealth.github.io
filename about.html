Frequently Asked Question

In Hong Kong, it is mandatory to get a doctor’s referral to see a therapist. If our therapist decides that you require a specialist doctor’s input after assessing your condition, you will be referred on accordingly. Some health insurers may also require either a GP or specialist doctor’s referral before they authorise coverage of therapy fees. Please check with your insurance company if you require any clarification.

During your first session, our therapist will obtain pertinent medical information from you and do an initial assessment of your condition. Our therapist will then customise a treatment plan and home exercise programme (if needed) based on your specific condition, symptoms and needs. Time will be taken to answer any questions you may have regarding your condition and will advise you on how you should manage and care for your condition. You should expect a good dose of education so that you will be empowered with the management of your condition as well!

We are able to accept MASTERCARD, VISA, AMEX, NETS, cash or PayNow.
